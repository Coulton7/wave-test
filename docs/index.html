Welcome to AESSEAL®

A specialist in the design and manufacture of mechanical seals and support systems.

Our Mechanical Seals are used in a wide range of pumps and rotating equipment worldwide to prevent liquids and gases escaping into the environment. We manufacture mechanical seal types to suit all industries and our investment in modular design means that we provide the best on-time delivery performance in the industry.

The AESSEAL® range of seals, seal support systems and bearing protectors are all designed to improve pump reliability and reduce maintenance costs. Our business is built around giving our customers such exceptional service that they need never consider alternative sources of supply.

AESSEAL® holds the world's most comprehensive standard inventory portfolio of mechanical seals, bearing protectors, seal support systems and packing.

AESSEAL® operates from 230 locations in 104 countries, including 9 manufacturing and 44 repair locations, and has more than 300 customer service representatives who visit industrial plants every day. FIND OUT MORE

 

Martin Lister

With over 40 years’ experience in the mechanical seal industry and 35 years of those at AESSEAL, Martin currently holds the role of International Pulp and Paper Manager and has worked with most of the major pulp and paper groups such as DS Smith, Mondi, Smurfit Kappa, International Paper Arjo Wiggins etc. Martin has helped the industry reduce total cost of ownership and increase the mean time between failure of its sealing solutions. Martin has an in depth knowledge of the rotating equipment and possesses a keen problem solving ability.
