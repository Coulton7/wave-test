Customer Portal

  • Environmental, social and corporate governance

  • Independent verification confirms net negative for Scope 1, 2 & 3 emissions for 2020

  • 64,200 tonnes of CO2e emissions avoided by using our Water Management System.

  • £1m charitable fund available to help fund community projects

  • Helping companies save 95 billion litres of water each year

  • Advanced Technology

  • Our world class manufacturing facilities use the latest in machine tool technology.

  • Over 7% of annual sales revenue has been reinvested in R&D over several decades.

  • Leading to the most advanced range of sealing technology available globally.

  • Customer Service

  • We aim to give such exceptional service that they need never seek another source of supply.

  • Quality is non-negotiable – it’s the thread that weaves together all our activities and people.

  • We aim to exceed expectation consistently: in delivery, quality, reliability and service.

  • We Care

  • We care about the environment, we care about reliability, we care about sustainability, we care about customer service

  • We Create

  • We create environmental and reliability improvement technology, we innovate, we create a culture of excellence

  • We Challenge

  • We challenge the “status quo”, we challenge our customers to think differently, we challenge the global mechanical seal market
